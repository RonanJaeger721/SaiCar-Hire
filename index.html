Welcome to Sai Car Hire

Sai Car Hire is a Harare-based car rental business providing affordable, reliable and convenient vehicle hire solutions. We help make your stay, business trip, family movement or out-of-town journey simpler from booking to handover.

Vehicle delivery can be arranged around Harare including the CBD, Avondale, Borrowdale, Mount Pleasant, Marlborough, Msasa, Highlands, Eastlea and Robert Gabriel Mugabe International Airport.
